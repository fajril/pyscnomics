Units and Currency
==================

This chapter defines the standard units and currency conventions used throughout
the pyscnomics documentation.

Standard Units
--------------

.. list-table:: Standard Units
   :header-rows: 1

   * - Measurement
     - Unit
     - Symbol
   * - Oil production
     - Thousand Stock Tank Barrels per year
     - MSTB/year
   * - Gas production
     - Billion Standard Cubic Feet per year
     - BSCF/year
   * - Currency
     - Thousand United States Dollar
     - MUSD
   * - Gross Heat Value
     - Billion BTU per Billion Standard Cubic Feet
     - BBTU/BSCF
   * - Time period
     - Year
     - year or yr
   * - Percentage
     - Decimal (0-1) or Percent
     - 0.15 or 15%

Currency Format
---------------

Throughout the documentation:

- Currency values are expressed in **MUSD** (Thousand United States Dollars)
- Large values use comma separators: 1,000 MUSD
- Decimal values use periods: 100.50 MUSD
- Example: $100,000 in the contract = 100 MUSD in pyscnomics notation
